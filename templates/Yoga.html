Why Yoga is Essential for Mental Health

Yoga combines physical postures, breathing techniques, and meditation to create powerful mental health benefits. Regular practice reduces stress hormones like cortisol, increases feel-good neurotransmitters, and activates the body's relaxation response. This ancient practice helps manage anxiety, depression, and daily stress while improving focus, emotional regulation, and overall mental clarity.
